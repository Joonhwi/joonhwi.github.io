I am a PhD candidate at Caltech. I am mainly interested in understanding gravitation in four dimensions. I have taken angles from scattering amplitudes and generalized symmetries. More broadly, I love to pursue geometrical approaches to physics.

During my PhD, I have worked on:

» Post-Minkowskian gravity to all orders in spin

» Massive twistor theory

» Gravitational instantons and double copy

» Generalized symmetry in gravity
