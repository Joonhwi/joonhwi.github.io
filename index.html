I am a theoretical physicist who likes to understand our universe from geometrical perspectives. I am a PhD candidate at Caltech.

#General_Relativity     #Scattering_Amplitudes     #Twistor_Theory

My time in California marks a productive 5-year period with 18 papers and 5 invited talks (more to come). I investigated various topics, from generalized symmetries, self-dual black holes, Newman-Janis algorithm, spinspacetime, massive twistor theory, worldline effective theory for post-Minkowskian gravity, scattering theory foundations (S-symplectomorphism, Magnusian), color-kinematics duality, ambitwistor worldlines, to differential geometry (Riemannian, symplectic, Poisson, Fedosov).
