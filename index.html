I am a PhD candidate at Caltech. My curiosities are spacetime, black holes, quantum particles/fields, and geometrical understandings of our universe. #General_Relativity #Scattering_Amplitudes #Twistor_Theory

My time in California marks a productive 5-year period with 18 papers and 5 invited talks (more to come). I investigated various topics, from color-kinematics duality, generalized symmetries, self-dual black holes, massive twistor theory, spinning black holes (Newman-Janis algorithm), post-Minkowskian gravity via worldline effective theory, scattering theory foundations (S-symplectomorphism, Magnusian), to differential geometry (Riemannian, symplectic, Fedosov).

Things are falling apart for me which is quite unfortunate but I hope I could be saved at the end.
